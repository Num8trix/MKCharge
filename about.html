ABOUT US

About MKRoofQuotes

We match Milton Keynes homeowners with vetted, local roofers — free, and with no obligation to proceed.

MKRoofQuotes exists to save Milton Keynes homeowners the hassle of ringing round roofers one by one, only to get wildly different prices and no way to compare them properly. Tell us about your job once, and we pass your details to a vetted local roofer who can give you a proper, written quote.

We're not a roofing company ourselves — we don't climb ladders or lay tiles. We're an introduction service that only works with NFRC or CompetentRoofer-registered roofers covering Milton Keynes and the surrounding towns and villages.

HOW IT WORKS

Three steps to a quote

01

Tell us about the job

Fill in a short form — what's wrong, your roof type, and how urgent it is. Takes about two minutes.

02

We match you with a roofer

We pass your details to one vetted local roofer suited to the job — no lists sold on to multiple companies.

03

Get a written quote

Your matched roofer surveys the job and gives you a proper written quote — no obligation to go ahead.
