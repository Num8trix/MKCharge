Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

This policy explains what personal information MKRoofQuotes ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use this website, why we collect it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over it.

In short: we collect the details you give us on our quote form so we can pass your enquiry to one matched roofer. We don't sell your data on to a list of companies — it goes to the single roofer we match you with for that job.

1. Who we are

MKRoofQuotes is a lead-generation website that introduces homeowners in Milton Keynes to independent, third-party roofing contractors. We are not a roofing contractor ourselves. You can contact us at mkroofquotes@gmail.com with any question about this policy or your data.

2. What information we collect

When you submit our quote form, we collect:

  • Your name, postcode, email address and phone number
  • Details about the job: roof type, roof age, the issue you're having, access, and how urgent it is
  • Any photos you choose to upload
  • Any additional information you type into the message field

We also collect some information automatically when you browse the site, via Google Analytics: pages visited, approximate location (derived from IP address), device and browser type, and how you arrived at the site. This is aggregated, standard web analytics data.

3. How we use your information

  • To match your enquiry with a suitable roofer in our network and pass your details to them so they can contact you with a quote
  • To respond to you directly if you contact us
  • To understand how visitors use the site and improve its content (via aggregated analytics)

We do not use your enquiry details for marketing purposes, and we do not sell your data to third-party marketing lists.

4. Our legal basis for processing

We rely on consent — you actively submit your details to request a quote — and on legitimate interests for basic site analytics needed to run and improve the website.

5. Who we share your data with

  • One matched roofer — the details from your enquiry are passed to a single roofer partner so they can provide your quote.
  • Formspree — the third-party service that processes and delivers our form submissions to us.
  • Google Analytics — for anonymised, aggregated website traffic statistics.

Some of these providers may process data on servers outside the UK/EEA. Where that happens, they do so under their own standard contractual safeguards.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry data only for as long as reasonably needed to make the introduction and to handle any follow-up query about that enquiry, and then delete it. Aggregated analytics data is retained in line with Google Analytics' standard retention settings.

7. Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to ask us for a copy of the data we hold about you, to correct it, to have it deleted, to restrict or object to how we use it, and to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you're unhappy with how we've handled your data. To exercise any of these, email mkroofquotes@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

This site uses cookies from Google Analytics to understand site traffic. You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings; this won't affect your ability to request a quote.

9. Children

This website and our services are intended for adults arranging work on their own property. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised.

11. Contact us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled: mkroofquotes@gmail.com.
