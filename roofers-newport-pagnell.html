Roofing services in Newport Pagnell

Common roofing issues we see in Newport Pagnell

  • Older town-centre properties — ageing slate or clay tile roofs often need repointing around chimneys or selective tile replacement rather than a full re-roof.
  • Storm and wind damage — exposed spots near the river and open estate edges can see slipped tiles after high winds.
  • Flat-roof extensions — common on rear extensions across the newer estates, and prone to felt or membrane wear after 10–15 years.
  • Gutter overflow — mature trees on older streets mean blocked gutters are a frequent call-out.
